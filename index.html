Nestle Australia
Product Analysis

The project aims to develop a product comparison dashboard to inform Nestlé's expansion plans. Microsoft PowerBi was utilized to analyze the performance of Nestlé's current products by examining total sales trends over the last three years, monthly sales patterns, and revenue variations across products. Additionally, it will identify locations with the highest and lowest sales. The objective is to provide data-driven recommendations to support strategic decisions for new product lines and enhance overall business growth.
